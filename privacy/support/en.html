FAQ

How do I restore my Premium?

Open the app → Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.

How can I manage/cancel my subscription?

iOS Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions, or App Store app → Account → Subscriptions.

I don’t receive notifications

iOS Settings → Notifications → Rutinim → Allow Notifications. Also check the in-app reminder toggle.

Delete all data

Rutinim → Settings → Data → Delete all data.
