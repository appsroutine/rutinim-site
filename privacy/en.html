Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-26 • Türkçe

Rutinim – Habit Tracker (“App”) helps you build routines. The developer does not collect your personal data for marketing, nor share it with third parties.

Data Collected/Used

  • In-app data (habit names, notes, emoji, completion logs) is stored on your device only.
  • Premium purchases are processed by the App Store; the developer never accesses payment card details.
  • Ads (free plan, Turkey only): An ad network (e.g., Google AdMob) may be used to serve ads. The provider may process device identifiers (e.g., IDFA/AAID), approximate location, and app/device interaction data for ad delivery, frequency capping, and debugging. The provider’s own privacy policies apply. The developer does not independently collect this data.

Personalization & Choices

  • On iOS, you may be asked for tracking permission (ATT). If you decline, ad personalization is limited or disabled.
  • You can adjust preferences in device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).
  • Upgrade to Premium for an ad-free experience.

Children’s Privacy

The App is for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Disclaimer

The App is a personal planning/tracking aid; no warranties are provided regarding accuracy, uninterrupted availability, or fitness for a particular purpose. Metrics and statistics are informational only.
