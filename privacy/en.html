Privacy Policy (EN)

Effective Date: 2025-01-01  |  Contact: appsroutine@gmail.com

1) Summary

Rutinim does not send your personal habit data to our servers for core features. Your data stays on device, and notifications are local. This document is for informational purposes and may change with app updates.

2) Data We Process

  • In-app data: Habit names/notes and daily completions are stored on your device.
  • Notifications: Local (on-device) reminders; no server processing for core notifications.
  • Purchases: Premium purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit / App Store.
  • Logs: Anonymous, aggregated technical data (e.g., crash reports) may be collected to improve reliability.

3) Ads & Analytics

Ads are generally possible in the app and are currently planned for the Türkiye version. Ads may be served by third-party providers, which might process minimal technical data (device identifiers, usage). Their own privacy policies apply. If analytics are integrated, only necessary and minimal data will be processed; this page will be updated accordingly.

4) Children’s Privacy

The app targets general audiences aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5) Your Choices

You can delete your on-device data anytime (Settings → Data → Delete all data). For any questions, email us at appsroutine@gmail.com.

6) Changes

This policy may be updated over time. We will update this page to reflect significant changes.

The app and this page are provided “as is” without warranties of any kind. The developer shall not be liable for any direct or indirect damages arising from the use of the app.
