Who we are

Data we collect & how we use it

  • On-device habit data (habit name, notes, emoji, planned days, completion logs) is stored locally on your device for the sole purpose of habit tracking. We do not upload or sell this data.
  • Notifications are scheduled locally based on your settings.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment details.
  • Advertising (AdMob): In certain regions (e.g., Türkiye) the free version may show ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google’s policies. Personalized ads require your iOS tracking permission (ATT). You can change ad tracking in iOS Settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Ad delivery (if shown) is provided by Google AdMob and governed by Google’s terms and policies.

Data retention

Your habit data remains on your device until you delete it (Settings → Delete all data) or uninstall the app.

Children’s privacy

Rutinim is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided data, contact us and we will assist.

Your choices

  • Manage notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Opt in/out of ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Delete in-app data from Settings → Data → Delete all data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected here with a new “Last updated” date.
